Pomodoro Timer

Front End

The Pomodoro technique is a time-management method developed by Francesco Cirillo in the late 1980s. The technique uses a timer to break down work into intervals, traditionally 25 minutes in length, separated by short breaks. Each interval is known as a pomodoro, from the Italian word for tomato, after the tomato-shaped kitchen timer that Cirillo used as a university student. Control state and component display to provide a quality experience and an accurate, reliable Pomodoro timer.

  • Dynamic state-based React components
  • Elegant UI design utilizing the Bootstrap framework
  • Hooks and state-management
