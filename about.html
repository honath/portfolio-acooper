About me

I've had an interest in coding for most of my life, and am working towards a degree in Computer Science.

One thing I've always enjoyed about coding has been the problem solving involved in it - always having a new task or problem, and being able to come up with a solution, whether it be a tried-and-true resolution or a brand new, unique answer, has always been exciting to me. I love being faced with new challenges and needing to figure out ways to overcome them on a daily basis.

Another aspect of coding that I am particularly fond of is the ability to create, whether it be a new software, a level in a game, or a website, creating has always been a part of my life, and I look forward to working on a team to create new and exciting things.

My interestes and hobbies outside of programming also tend to include creativity in creation, and problem solving in the form of implementing my idea, or overcoming limitations, such as specialty tools.

A great example is my woodworking - something as simple as the necklace above took some creativity, as I lacked many of the tools that would have made carving it easy. My other "crafting" hobby is blacksmithing - as for the less crafty ones, I'm interested in things like hiking, kayaking, and climbing, and I enjoy playing video games and watching anime.
