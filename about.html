Who are you?

My name is Alex, and I'm currently 23 years old.

I'm based out of Charlotte, North Carolina, however I was born in Washington state, raised primarily in Florida, and have lived in both Los Angeles, California and Atlanta, Georgia.

I originally attended university in pursuit of a Computer Science degree with a minor in Japanese, however I also have some interest in later expanding my knowledge and learning both Electrical and Mechanical engineering.

I hope to one day apply all of my knowledge, combined with some of my hobbies, to create wonderful projects to share with the world.

What are your interests?

I have some more dedicated hobbies and interests that I'll be getting to later on, but there are a few that are easier to get into and keep up with.

The first is, like many others, playing video games. Early on (even before middle school!) I would play games like World of Warcraft, maining a protection paladin, or flying jets in Battlefield 3 as an ace pilot. I think this is where I may have started to develop an appreciation for software and programming.

Another major interest of mine is anime! They are, essentially, just Japanese cartoons - but they can be so much more. The range of genres that the term "anime" encompasses is almost as extensive as the list of genres themselves, and I highly encourage anybody to find a show they'd like to start with. I'm always more than happy to recommend some, as well!

Last but not least is playing guitar. I don't get to do it as much as I used to, but my favorite songs to play are Blackbird by The Beatles, Small Bump by Ed Sheeran, and Cherry Wine by Hozier. I play entirely without a guitar pick, preferring to use just my fingers.

Woodworking

One of my bigger hobbies is woodworking - though this can mean many different things to different people.

For me, woodworking means crafting beautiful (and functional) furniture, with the hopes of it lasting for a lifetime or more. I have, however, done some hand carving a well, the first of which can be seen in the adjacent image.

One of the ways I want to incorporate all of my skills with my woodworking, is by creating custom parts, such as wooden gears or latches, and just seeing what I can do from there!

Right now, everything I've done with woodworking has been through the use of hand-tools and elbow grease only.

Blacksmithing

Much like my woodworking, I hope to connect everything together with my blacksmithing as well.

I unfortunately haven't had any opportunities to practice this lately, though I hope to continue back up in the near future, and to make some really cool things!
